How It Works

FreshMark is a small, digital label that attaches to any food container. It shows what’s inside, when it was stored, and when to use it — all on a clear e-ink display. No more forgotten leftovers or messy marker labels!

Features

Reusable

Stick or strap it onto any container. It’s waterproof and dishwasher-safe.

Smart Display

E-ink display shows food name, storage date, and expiration clearly.

App Sync

Use the companion app to track reminders and get freshness alerts.

Eco-Friendly

Helps reduce food waste and cuts down on disposable plastic stickers.
